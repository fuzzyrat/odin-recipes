Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, also known by the pural form lasagne, is a type of pasta, possible of the oldest types, made in very wide, flat sheets.

Ingredients

For the meat sauce

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chunk
  • 1/2 medium onoin, diced
  • 1/2 large bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 3 ounce tomato paste
  • 1 can of crushed tomato
  • 2 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano or 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley, packed
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder and/or garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon red or white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon to 1/4 cup sugar
  • Salt to taste

To assemble the lasagna

  • 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodes
  • 15 ounce ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 pounds mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 pound fresh grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Put pasta water on to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell pepper, onions, and garlic; add back the beef
  4. Make the sauce
  5. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles
  6. Preheat the oven at 375 degrees
  7. Assemble the lasagna
  8. Bake
  9. Cool and serve
